Akan Names

A web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.
Here is a list of both male and female Akan names depending on the day of the week they were born.

Days of Week Male Female
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwado Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ama
